\section{Conclusion}

In this paper, we proposed the Cascade Engine, an intelligent, multi-tier routing framework designed to optimize LLM inference. By leveraging deterministic preprocessing and learned routing strategies, our system significantly curtails operational costs without a proportional loss in response quality. The integration of local privacy filtering and semantic caching further bolsters the system's viability for enterprise deployments. Future work will explore non-stationary routing environments and the dynamic auto-scaling of local tiers based on real-time budget and latency constraints.
